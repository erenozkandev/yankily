Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

💬
Chat Companions

Create companions directly from your own chat exports. Your history becomes the foundation of a living connection.

🎛
Fine-tuned Style

Adjust tone, reply timing, emoji use, memory, and guardrails. Shape exactly how each companion communicates.

📥
One Inbox

All your companion conversations in a single clean inbox. Organized, searchable, always at your fingertips.

🎙
Rich Messaging

Send text, photos, and voice notes. The full range of expression you'd use in any real conversation.

☁️
Synced Everywhere

Manage companions and settings from one workspace. Your account stays synced across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

🛡
Consent by Design

Built for personal, consent-based use. Guardrails let you control privacy and sensitive reply boundaries.

How It Works

Up and running
in minutes.

1
Export your chat

Export a conversation from WhatsApp, iMessage, or any messaging app in just a few taps.

2
Import & create

Upload the export in Yankily. The app reads your chat history to build your companion's unique voice.

3
Tune the style

Adjust tone, reply timing, language, and guardrails from the Workspace until it feels just right.

4
Start talking

Open the chat and go. Send text, photos, or voice notes. Your companion is always ready.

Apply Tuning

Shape exactly how each companion communicates. From the words they use to how fast they reply — every detail is in your hands.

  • 🎭
    Tone & Expression

    Choose between Faithful (true to source), Balanced, or Expressive communication modes.

  • ⏱
    Reply Timing

    Set replies to Instant, Quick, Natural, or Slow — matching how that person actually texted.

  • 🌐
    Language Detection

    Auto-detect from your chat history, or lock the language to Turkish, English, and more.

  • 🛡
    Guardrails

    Control privacy and sensitive reply limits for a safe, personal experience on your terms.
